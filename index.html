About
Who I Am

I am Frandly Ilham, a web developer focused on building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I enjoy turning ideas into functional digital experiences with clear purpose and thoughtful design.

In every project, I pay attention not only to visual appearance but also to code structure and user experience. I believe a good website should look professional, work smoothly across all devices, and be easy to maintain and develop in the future.

I primarily work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and I continuously improve my skills to deliver better results. For me, a website is more than just an online presence—it’s a tool to help individuals and businesses grow online.
